About Me

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Department of Civil and Costal Engineering, University of Florid, , majoring in Transportation Engineering (Please find my lab here). I received my bachelor degree in Transportation Engineering from Southwest Jiao Tong University, Chengdu, China, where I was advised by Prof. Hongtai Yang. Before joining UF, I was a visiting research assistant at New York University Shanghai, Shanghai, China from 2020 to 2021, where I was advised by Prof. Zhibin Chen.

My research mainly focuses on the applications of data science (e.g., data analytics and machine learning) on travel behavior and evacuation behavior.
